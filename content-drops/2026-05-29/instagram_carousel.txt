SLIDE 2
The whole Summer House reunion came down to:

WHEN exactly did Amanda develop feelings for West?

Team Ciara: "She was feeling it the WHOLE time."
Team Amanda: "Nothing crossed a line until Kyle and I were done."

Both sides want a timestamp.

That's the problem.

SLIDE 3
Here's what romantic partnership requires:

A before. And an after.
A moment when it was innocent.
A moment when it wasn't.

Find the line.
Assign the blame.

This is how the whole system works.

---

SLIDE 4
Here's what human feelings actually do:

They develop gradually.
In the background.
Without permission.
Without a notification.

There is no internal calendar invite when friendship tips into something else.

---

SLIDE 5
And yet we've built romantic partnership around the rule:

"There must have been a moment when you knew."

If you didn't flag that moment? You did something wrong.

This rule creates accountability.
It's also factually incompatible with how attachment works.
